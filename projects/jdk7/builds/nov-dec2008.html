JDK 7 builds and integrations

This is the schedule for builds and integrations of JDK 7.

Each day is divided into two integration slots, which are periods of time when a specific group is scheduled to integrate its pending changes into the JDK 7 master forest.

AM (morning) 1:00am to 2:00pm US/Pacific
PM (afternoon) 2:00pm to 10:00pm US/Pacific
